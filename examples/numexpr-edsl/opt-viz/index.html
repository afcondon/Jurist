Provably optimal

A 0/1 knapsack — pick items to maximize value without exceeding the weight budget — described once in PureScript and handed to Julia's JuMP/HiGHS mixed-integer solver. The obvious greedy heuristic (computed in pure PureScript) grabs the best value-per-weight item and stalls. The solver doesn't guess — its branch-and-bound proves the global optimum, closing the gap to zero. There is no MILP solver in the JS/WASM world to hand a model to.
