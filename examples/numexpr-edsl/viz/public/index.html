Double pendulum

A double pendulum in Cartesian coordinates is an index-1 differential-algebraic system: the rod tensions are algebraic variables, closed by the rigidity constraints. A typed PureScript description crossed the seam once; ModelingToolkit denoted it, derived the constraints, and an implicit stiff solver integrated it on the Julia runtime — holding the rods rigid to ~1e-8 through fully chaotic motion. This page is a pure player of the resulting frames: the browser draws what it could never have computed.
